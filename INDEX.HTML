About Us

Chemaner Vocational Training Centre is committed to equipping the youth with hands-on skills for self-reliance and employment. We offer a range of technical and vocational courses designed to meet the needs of the local and national economy.

Courses Offered

  • Masonry
  • ICT (Information and Communication Technology)
  • Hairdressing
  • Fashion Design
  • Motor Vehicle Mechanics
  • Carpentry
